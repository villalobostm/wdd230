Overview

Site Name

Santa Tecla Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Santa Tecla Chamber of Commerce permanently promotes and defends the system of free initiative, and business development with social responsibility, leading actions and facilitating services that promote the competitiveness of our associates.

Scenarios

  • What virtual courses do you offer with financial topics, interpersonal skills, sales, and marketing?
  • What has been the growth in recent years of the chamber's members?
  • What are the requirements and necessary steps to be able to join as a member of the chamber?
